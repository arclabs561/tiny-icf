## Research Summary

Based on research of similar projects (wordfreq, gensim, etc.) and naming conventions:

### Common Patterns
- **Size prefixes**: `tiny-`, `nano-`, `micro-`, `mini-`, `light-`, `compact-`
- **Function suffixes**: `-icf`, `-freq`, `-est`, `-model`
- **Format**: `{size}-{function}` or `{function}-{size}`

### Similar Projects
- **wordfreq**: Established library for word frequency (40+ languages)
- **gensim**: General NLP library with frequency-aware models
- Pattern: Clear, descriptive, professional names
